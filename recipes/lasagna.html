Lasagne Verdi alla Bolognese Recipe

    Ingredients (Ragu)
  • 1 package mincetta, minced
  • 1/2 cup diced carrot
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 7 ounces ground beef
  • 6 ounces ground pork
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine
  • 1,5 cups plain tomato sauce
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or more as needed
    Ingredients (Pasta)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 package frozen spinach
  • 1/2 package gluten-free all-purpose baking flour
  • 2 medium eggs, at room temperature
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
    Ingredients (Bechamel Sauce)
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup gluten-free all-purpose baking flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
